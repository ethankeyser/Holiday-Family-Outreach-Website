Adams County Community Foundation Giving Spree! Thursday, November 3, 2022

posted Sep 23, 2022 By Julie Keyser

Drop off Your HFO Donation:

Bring your completed Giving Spree Donation Form and gift to the Gettysburg Times on November 3 between 1:00 p.m. and 5:00 p.m. for curbside giving. You’ll get a personal thank-you and a friendly wave from our Holiday Family Outreach Board Members!

Mail in Your HFO Donation:

Complete the Giving Spree Donation Form for Holiday Family Outreach and mail it with your gift to: Adams County Community Foundation, 25 South 4th St. Gettysburg, PA 17325. Please mail your donation early to ensure we have your gift in time for the Giving Spree on November 3rd.

Donate Online via the ACCF Website:

Visit the Adams County Community Foundation website on November 3 any time between 12:01 a.m. and 12:00 midnight to complete your HFO donation online. It’s quick, convenient and secure!
