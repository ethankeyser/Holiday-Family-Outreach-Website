What is Holiday Family Outreach?

Since 1953, Holiday Family Outreach, an all-volunteer nonprofit 501(c)3 organization, has provided Christmas toys and food to low-income families in Adams County, Pennsylvania.

Goal for 2022


Our goal is to raise $60,000 for food certificates allowing us to provide $50 food certificates for 1,300 referrals. Food certificates are offered to low-income families and elderly residents through the Office of Aging. We also accept toy donations! Toy donations can be dropped off at United Way or you can make your donation payable to: Holiday Family Outreach, Inc and mail to : PO Box 4013, Gettysburg, PA 17325 or Donate Here Thank You!
